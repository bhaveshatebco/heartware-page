ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Ebco Heartware is a Reward Program exclusively designed for contractors & carpenters associated with Ebco. Contractors & carpenters can earn points by scanning QR codes available on a wide range of products. The accumulated points can be redeemed for exciting rewards. This program acknowledges the valuable role contractors & carpenters play in promoting and using Ebco products at project sites and rewards them for their continued engagement.

EARN AS YOU WORK

Whenever you purchase select Ebco products, simply Scan the QR code available on them using the App.

Each scan earns you points automatically, turning your daily work into opportunities for rewards.

HOW TO JOIN

You can join easily via:

Self-Registration

Download our Ebco Heartware App from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Follow the simple on-screen steps to register yourself.

Referral by Registered Users

Already know someone who’s a registered member of our Reward Program?

They can refer you directly through the App.

Registration via Sales Officer

Our on-ground sales officers are always ready to assist. Simply connect with a sales officer in your area, they will help you fill out the form, upload the necessary documents, and complete your registration seamlessly.
